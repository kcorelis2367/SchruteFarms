About Us

We are a locally owned farm and bed and breakfast, right outside of Sctanton, Pennsylvania. Our farm has many fun activities and tours to keep you and your family busy for the duration of your stay.

Activities and Tours

  • Plowing Fields
  • Making Tables
  • Beet Wine Classes
  • Mattress Making
  • Tour of the fields, barn, or slaughterhouse
  • Mose's table making demonstration
  • Mattress Making
  • Beet Planting

Our Crop

Harvesting season for our beets is typicaly from mid-July to late-November, although is varries from year to year. be sure to purchase some to take home as you check-out.

Locally Owned

Our humble beet farm has been passed down by the Schrute family for many generations. It continues to be a locally owned and operated business. We hope you will love our farm as much as we do.
